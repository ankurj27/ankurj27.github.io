Healthcare Service Provider Dashboard

Turning Healthcare Data into Meaningful Insights.

❮ ❯

Overview

This dashboard provides a high-level summary of healthcare billing, treatment costs, insurance coverage, and departmental performance across multiple cities. It helps decision-makers understand cost distribution, identify high-revenue procedures, and monitor patient billing patterns.

Key Insights

  • Total Billing: $3M with an average billing of $675 per visit-indicating balanced service utilization across facilities.
  • Medication Cost: $546K, averaging $109 per patient, showing medication contributes moderately to overall expenses.
  • Insurance Coverage: $2M, reducing out-of-pocket costs to $1M, suggesting good insurance penetration among patients.
  • Room Charges: $180K - relatively lower, reflecting short stays or efficient bed turnover.

Regional Trends

  • Cities like Edinburgh, Birmingham, and Sheffield show higher billing amounts, aligning with higher patient volumes and specialized services.

Procedural Insights

  • Diagnostic services like X-ray ($1.05M) and CT Scan ($805K) contribute over 50% of total billing, highlighting diagnostics as key revenue drivers.

Department-Wise Overview

  • Cardiology ($0.85M) and Orthopedics ($0.81M) are top contributors, likely due to high-complexity and high-demand procedures.
  • Pediatrics and Neurology show moderate costs, balancing overall hospital load.

    • Diagnosis & Service Type Mix

    Fracture and Hyptertension are the most common cases across emergency and inpatient services, reflecting typical hospital case patterns.

The Takeaway

  • This dashboard provides a unified view of healthcare costs — enabling administrators to identify high-cost areas, optimize department budgets, and improve care delivery without compromising affordability.

Tools Used

  • Power BI Desktop, Google Sheet
