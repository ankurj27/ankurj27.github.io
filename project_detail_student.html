Student Performance Analysis Dashboard

A Comprehensive View of Student Performance Across Subjects, Time, and Topics

❮ ❯

Overview

The Student Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of student performance across Math and Science, analyzed by month-wise marks, topic-wise marks, and top-performing students. It helps identify performance trends, subject strengths, topic gaps, and consistent achievers, enabling data-driven academic decisions.

Key Insights

  • Science Outperforms Math Overall
    • Across months and topics, Science shows higher aggregate scores, indicating stronger student engagement or understanding compared to Math.
  • Performance Peaks Mid-Year
    • Both Math and Science demonstrate noticeable score spikes in the middle months, suggesting effective teaching cycles or better student preparedness during this period.
  • Foundational Topics Drive Scores
    • Core topics like Fundamentals, Motion, and Polynomials contribute the highest cumulative marks, highlighting their importance in overall performance.
  • Consistent High Performers Across Subjects
    • Students like Reet Gupta and Arhat Jain appear among the top performers in both Math and Science, reflecting consistent academic excellence.

Retention & Value Metrics

  • Month-wise Math Performance
    • Math scores show moderate fluctuations with clear peaks during select months. Some students demonstrate steady improvement, while others show inconsistent performance, indicating varied learning curves.
  • Month-wise Science Performance
    • Science exhibits stronger and more stable monthly performance compared to Math. Several months record significantly higher totals, suggesting better conceptual clarity or assessment alignment.
  • Math Topic-wise Analysis
    • Topics such as Polynomials, Linear Equations, and Number Systems contribute the most marks. Advanced or less-practiced topics show lower totals, indicating potential learning gaps.
  • Science Topic-wise Analysis
    • Fundamentals, Motion, and Matter dominate topic-wise scores. More complex or specialized topics (e.g., Gravitation, Tissues) record lower marks, signaling areas for reinforcement.
  • Top 5 Students-Math
    • The top Math performers are led by Arhat Jain and Reet Gupta, followed by Advik Kansal, Raghav Aggarwal, and Adhiraj Aggarwal, showing a competitive but close score range.
  • Top 5 Students-Science
    • Reet Gupta tops Science with a clear margin, followed by Arhat Jain and Saara Aggarwal. The spread indicates strong subject mastery among the leading students.

The Takeaway

  • The dashboard highlights strong Science performance, mid-year academic momentum, and the critical role of foundational topics in driving results. Targeted support for weaker topics and leveraging best practices from top performers can further improve overall student outcomes.
