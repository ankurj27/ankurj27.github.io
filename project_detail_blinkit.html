Blinkit Sales & Performance Insights Dashboard

Analyze Blinkit's retail sales data to identify growth opportunities and consumer behavior patterns.

❮ ❯

Overview

This dashboard captures Blinkit's performance metrics — from total sales to outlet-level insights. It tells a story of how data can reveal what customers truly value in everyday shopping.

The Big Picture

  • $1.2M in total sales —a strong pulse of consistent customer trust.
  • 8,523 items sold —every item, a tiny story of choice.
  • $141 average sale value —steady purchasing power across outlets.
  • 3.9 average rating —satisfaction that keeps customers coming back.

Where the Sales Are Coming From

  • Supermarket Type 1 leads with $787K in sales, contributing over 65% of revenue.
  • Tier 3 cities outperform Tier 1 & 2 with $472K in sales —smaller towns, bigger opportunities.
  • Grocery Stores trail at $151K,signaling room for better visibility and engagement.
  • Large outlets outperform with $507.9K —showing scalability works when backed by variety.

What Customers Love Most

  • Top Categories: Fruits & Snack Foods (~$180K each) — the perfect balance between healthy and indulgent.
  • Fat Content Preference: Regular-fat products rule with $776K, nearly double low-fat sales.

    Comfort food always wins — even in the data.
  • Item Visibility: Low visibility in smaller stores (0.10) = untapped potential.

Growth Over Time (2012-2022)

  • Steady climbacross the decade, peaking at $205K in 2018.
  • Post-2018:stable growth, signaling maturity and brand reliability.
  • Blinkit's evolution reflects consistency, adaptability, and smart scaling.

Key Insights for Strategy

  • Tier 3 expansion =highest potential for future growth.
  • Improve item visibility to boost smaller outlet sales.
  • Regular-fat & snack items =consistent customer favorites.
  • Large outlets deliver higher ROI —sustain and replicate this model.
  • Balanced product mix(health + indulgence) = customer retention.

The Takeaway

  • Blinkit's data tells more than just performance metrics —it's a story of how everyday choices shape business growth.
  • From metros to Tier 3 towns, customers show that convenience, comfort, and trust drive success. When data meets storytelling, insight becomes strategy.
